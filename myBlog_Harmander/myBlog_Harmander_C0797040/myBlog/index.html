About Me

Welcome to my Blog.
Let me briefly introduce you to my Mini World through this Blog. Hope you enjoy the Tour. Cheers!!

My Name is Harmander Singh S/o S. Daljit Singh and Satnam Kaur. I am born and brought up in Punjab,India a land of seven rivers and rich in culture .

Regarding my qualification, I have completed my gradution from RIMT-IET Mandi Gobindgarh College and I am currently pursing my post graduation from Lambton College.

I am a simple person who beleives in simple living and high thinking. I normally spend my free time reading and exploring new things.Like, I watch space documentaries, do star gazing, I also help my mother in her house hold chores and spend some time with my friends.
I am passionate for photography and also create digital painting sometimes. I have shared my clicks in this blog. Hope you like my Work.

I have immense love and respect for my homeland Punjab, India. It is known for its brave, helpful and hospitable people, and their exuberant festivals and celebrations.

Punjabis love everything that is over the top, colourful dresses, foot tapping music, and an undying love for food and drinks! I will show you the beauty of punjab and my likes and dislikes through this website.

Places I Visted and Must Visit Places in India

The top Places to visit in Punjab are Amritsar, Chandigarh, Ludhiana, Patiala, Pathankot and many more places to visit. Punjab is the best place for the pilgrims and the travellers to visit, it is the most attraction Point for the visitors to visit in Punjab. It is also known as the ‘Land Of Five Rivers’.

  • Amritsar
    • Golden Temple, Akal Takht
    • Jalian Wala Bagh
  • Chandigarh
    • Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake

  • Ludhiana
    • Forts - Lodhi, Payal, Phillaur
